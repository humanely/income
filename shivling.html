Shape of Shivling

As succinctly explained earlier, Shivling is outer shape symbol derived from polar coordinate representation of Maheshwar Sutra. The forms one witnesses in temples and creatives are artistic and symmetric form of Shivling. The outer shape is made up of 14 points, which are respectively the positions of It-markers in Maheshwar Sutras. The inner space of Shivling contains the maheshwar sutra in an ordered arrangement which has inherent meaning and reasons to stay at particular location as per the Sutras. A deeper study also clears the doubts as raised by Katyayana(in Madhav Deshpande, 1997), for appearance of h(ह) twice or order of elements in fifth sutra. Like Shiva, Maheshwar Sutra is supreme truth. Shivling is supreme truth. By theory of induction, every entity linked to Shiva is absolute truth.

Why is it called Ling?

As we have already put forth our core theory that Shivling is the inherent meaning of maheshwar sutra in coordinate system, it can also be used to derive analytic functions to explain the world phenomena, e.g, standard model of quantum physics. But the name of Ling given has no relation to phallic, as propound by dark age theories.

Ling in sanskrit, means symbol.

To understand the reason of naming the maheshwar sutra model as Ling, we must understand the ontology of that time. Unlike our current time, the past era had lesser taxonomical relations to sex organs. The mention of sex organs could not have created negative connotations of mischief or sin, but only a well understood human part which has greater role, than ours in limited ontological society. By limited ontology, I mean to highlight the branches of knowledge in physical materialistic world, and also it signifies that the past ontology would have been far more deeper in spiritual body of knowledge. Whereas, our current spiritual ontology is obviously far less shallower, given that we have lost a humongous treasure of that culture and knowledge. And it is the sole reason, that naming sutra model as Shivling, signified the essence of Lord Shiva as creator of universe and all seeds of creations come from him.

Manifestation of Shivling in universe - Shivling is everywhere

It is imperative to ask in material sense to manifest Shivling around us, at least for proofs. Since Shivling has been made most sacred means of worship and one where Shiva resides himself, Shivling has to be a physical manifestation of something which must be existing in every particle of the universe. And it is indeed so, as Shivling represents all the forces of our nature arranged in an order so that one can truly derive detailed knowledge. State of every particle is described by the Maheswar Sutra and as such Shivling is an analytic representation of those formula.

Creation of Universe

The creation of this universe as well as all others, has happened through the sounds generated by damaru of Shiva. And as such, Shivling is sacred in all universes. And all universes are created and destroyed by Shiva. Therefore, it is imperative that different universes are governed by different principles, but universal constants are indeed decided by order of sounds produced by the daaru of Shiva. Therefore, sacredness of Shivling is linked to sacredness of knowledge of creation of every particle in this universe.
